Visit Santa

Santa and his elves are back again for 2022!

What better way to treat the kids than visiting Santa at Castleisland Christmas Market! Treat the little ones by paying a visit to the jolly man in red himself.

As always Santa Claus will know some special information about your children and take some gorgeous photos to capture the magic of meeting Santa!

Please note that coming to see Santa is free to all, however he is a busy man and is not guaranteed to have time to meet everyone, so please arrive nice and early to avoid dissapointment. Santa visiting experiences run from Thursday to Sundays and from 12pm to 7pm.

Food & Drinks

Variety of choices between homegrown and European cuisine

Food lovers rejoice as there is cheese of every kind but not only that, you can feast on a bratwurst, hot-dog or 100% Irish beef hamburger. Or they can indulge in French pastries, pretzels, confectionery, crêpes and waffles

A hot chocolate is a real crowd pleaser especially in the cold weather.

For the adults we also have a range of hot beverages that might tickle your fancy. Treat yourself to a mulled wine, hot cider or a hot whiskey/port.

Handcrafted Decorations

Add a personal touch to a gift this season

With some of the finest craftspeople from the country we are sure you will find that perfect gift for someone special

The market has numerous stall full of unique handmade christmas ornaments from baubles, miniture figurines and dolls to list just a few.

Every stall takes cash and card so do not fret about having to run to the atm once you see set your eyes on a piece of art from our talented merchants.

Games & Carolling

Have some fun with the family and enjoy our carnival games or listen to our amazing choirs.

The market has a range of stalls where you can test out your skills with the chance of winning a variety of items such as teddys, sweets and cool gadgets.

Or come visit on a Friday evening after 5pm where there will be an almalgamation of schools performing Christmas carols, which is sure to put everyone in the festive spirit. The gorgeous singers are from the local schools around the area and the diversity in age ranges from 12 up to 18. These are one talented group of pupils and not to be missed.

Cookie decorating & Face painting

Let the children express themselves whether it be how they design their cookie or what character they get painted on their face.

In our Cookie Decoration workshop the children get to help Mrs. Claus decorate some home baked sugar cookies. There are a plethora of icings and toppings to chose from. These workshops are available all week from 6pm to 7pm and cost €5 per child. Please ring the number or email the email address below for more details.

As for the face painting, we have gifted artists on hand to transform your children into animals, superheroes, princesses, you name it. It is sure to add a bit of colour and a smile to your childs face.
