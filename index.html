Seasons greetings from Kerry's most exciting Christmas Markets. Legend has it that Elfis McTwinkles, one of Santa's retired elves, chose to live in the Magical town of Castleisland, and when he arrived he helped set up this market to make him feel at home every Christmas. This will be the markets twelfth year of spreading joy and festive spirit as it continues to grow.

Castleisland Markets have had thousands of people from around the world wander through its stalls just to catch a glimpse of the magic.

Come and see for yourself!

We have something for everyone

There are many stalls where you can buy local Irish produce, gourmet food, hot chocolate and handmade decorations made by local artisans. You can even combat the cold season with a cup of mulled wine or cider!

In addition, Santa Claus comes every year to meet the children. He meets them in “Santa’s village”, which is set up especially for him in Castleisland. On top of all this we have cookie decorating, face painting and carollers. A festive atmosphere awaits, to the great joy of young and old alike!
